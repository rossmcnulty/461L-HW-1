SEC AO Application

December 11, 2013 - 4:20pm — Ross

Are you interested in becoming part of our team at IEEE? We have an open Assistant Officer position for SEC next semester and are accepting applications!

Assistant Officers, as you might expect, assist their senior officer with their tasks. The SEC AO will be expected to attend Student Engineering Council meetings next semester and help put on Engineering Week this February, so familiarity with E-Week is preferred (but not required!).

This position is open to any national student member of IEEE. If you aren't yet a national member of IEEE you can register here.
